CoMo Through the Years

Columbia has changed significantly since 1949, when the city was documented by photographers as part of a workshop. Current photographs reflect much of that change. Less visible changes like the drastic increases in minimum wage and population are no less important to note. And yet, even after 66 years, some things have not changed at all (see left).
